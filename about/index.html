--- Lee Clemon --- I am a design engineer, sustainable and additive manufacturing professional, and tenacious problem solver. I consult on manufacturing and design challenges as well as conduct individual research. My work present work targets:
  1. Environmental, social, and economic assessment and improvement of products and manufacturing processes
  2. Design engineering for mechanical, pressurized, and hazardous processes
  3. Additive manufacturing process improvement and optimization
  4. Materials processing and pathways for a more circular economy
In addition, I serve the American Society of Mechanical Engineers both in codes and standards for pressure vessels and in developing volunteer leaders. Published works may be found on a variety of platforms: [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=fVLB6psAAAAJ&hl=en&authuser=1)
